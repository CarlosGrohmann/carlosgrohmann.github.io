ISG Council

The ISG Council meets multiple times per year to discuss the matters of the ISG. Meetings follow pre-established agendas and detailed notes are taken and archived. All members of the ISG can access the archives and all documentation on the functioning of the ISG upon request to the Council by email: geomorphometry.org@gmail.com.
