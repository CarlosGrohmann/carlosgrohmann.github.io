Community

You can have your script/software/dataset listed in this page and help the whole community! Just post your files on a repository such as Zenodo or GitHub, write a blog post describing your work (see instructions here) and contact us via e-mail or our contact form so we can update the list and have your post published.

If you choose to host your data in Zenodo, we have an open community that you can select when uploading your files:

  • Geomorphometry Community
