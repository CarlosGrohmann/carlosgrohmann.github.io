Welcome

Hello there. Thanks for stopping by. Here you can find a bit more on what I do, my research, software I develop and a bunch of other stuff.

I’m an Associate Professor of Geotechnologies at the Institute of Energy and Environment of the University of São Paulo, Brazil, where I run the Spatial Analysis and Modelling Lab (SPAMLab).

My research interests include Digital Terrain Analysis (Geomorphometry), DEMs, GIS, UAVs, LiDAR, Landslides, Remote Sensing, Free Software (FOSS), Morphotectonics/Neotectonics, and Karstic Systems.
