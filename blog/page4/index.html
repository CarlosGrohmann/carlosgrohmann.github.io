resample and derive or derive and resample? [paper review]

Working with Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) have became a standard practice in scientific disciplines related to the physical environment, from geomorphology, geology, to biogeography and climate modeling. The Shuttle Radar Topography Mission (SRTM - Farr et al., 2007) and ASTER GDEM (Tachikawa et al., 2011) provide a near-global to global coverage at medium spatial resolution (30 to 90m) free of charge, while WorldDEM (Krieger et al., 2009) is a global dataset with 12m resolution and ALOS World3D (Tadono et al., 2014) delivers a global DEM with only 5m resolution.
